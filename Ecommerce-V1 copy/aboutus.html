About Flora & Fauna

Welcome to Flora & Fauna! We are passionate about all things green and believe that plants have the power to transform any space into a peaceful and thriving oasis. Our collection of indoor and outdoor plants are carefully sourced from local growers and nurseries to ensure the highest quality and health of each plant. Whether you're a seasoned plant enthusiast or just starting to cultivate your green thumb, we offer a wide variety of plants to suit every level of experience and personal taste. From low maintenance succulents and air plants, to show-stopping tropicals and rare finds, we have something for everyone. In addition to plants, we also offer a selection of unique and stylish planters, terrariums, and other accessories to help you create the perfect display for your plants. Our knowledgeable staff is always available to answer any questions and offer tips and advice on plant care and maintenance. Thank you for choosing our plant store as your go-to destination for all things green. We look forward to helping you bring the beauty and vitality of plants into your home and life.

Our Values

We are a 3rd Generation Family Business that has been growing plants since the 1970's on the same property we still grow house plants on today. We used to operate strictly wholesale before 2019, but since then, we have shifted entirely to serving our customers online.

Our mission is to grow high quality vigorous house plants & ship them quickly and safely from our greenhouse to your house, with an unmatched customer experience.

High quality soils, extended time-release granular fertilizer and locally sourced nursery pots means a better growing experience for the plants in our greenhouse and hopefully a smile on your face when your plants have arrived home.
